When a person is stress-free and peaceful, naturally they want to make a difference in the live of others.

Human values do not depend on, and are not derived from, any external authority.

As the infinite potentiality within all people, human values are already present in every human being; they need only be rekindled to thrive and grow.

There is a close relationship between human rights and human values - Rekindling human values throughout the world is essential to achieving universal human rights, peace within individuals, harmonious coexistence among different peoples and cultures, and security on the planet.

Through cultivating the practice of human values, the enhanced experience of inner peace results in outer dynamism which impacts individuals, the community and the planet.

Our projects are brought to life by individuals who are living human values. When a person is stress-free and peaceful, they naturally want to make a difference in the lives of others and IAHV is a platform for these projects.

3. Planet

To meet the global challenges of climate change, IAHV offers inclusive and sustainable development interventions including disaster relief, river rejuvenation and providing access to renewable energy.

Human Rights & Peace Building

Fostering human values which unite us as a global community, IAHV has initiated peace building programs to resolve long-standing conflicts across the globe, achieving lasting peace through dialogue, advocacy and service to humanity.

Afforestation

Integrated afforestation and watershed development projects combating the climate change threat and increasing green cover, provide a large natural and sustainable part of the solution to reduce global warming.

Renewable Energy
